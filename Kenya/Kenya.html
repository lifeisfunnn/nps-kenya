My Favorite National Parks in Kenya

Kenya is a country with very diverse landscapes, flora and fauna. There are many exciting things to do, from snorkelling in the Indian Ocean to climbing Mount Kenya (the second-highest mountain in Africa!). It is very difficult to choose, but these are my three favorite national parks in Kenya:

  1. Aberdare National Park

    2. You can do a great number of hikes with magical landscapes here. 'Dragon's Teeth' is an impressive formation of rocks that you can see in one day without going up too high in altitude.

  2. Amboseli National Park

    3. The park is a relatively popular destination for tourists, and understandably so. The savannah and its inhabitants are stunning. You can even see Kilimanjaro on a good day!

  3. Meru National Park

    4. Much less frequently visited by tourists, this is a great place to enjoy the scenery and see animals close up without anyone else around. The surrounding leave you feeling a great appreciation for nature.

By the way, the Kenya Wildlife Service protects its national parks from poachers and illegal loggers.
